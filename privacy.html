1. Overview

Lishe processes personal and health-related information to deliver personalized meal planning for certified nutrition professionals and individuals. We are committed to protecting user data and using it only for lawful and clearly stated purposes.

2. Data We Collect

For individuals using Lishe Planner, we may collect name, phone number, anthropometrics, dietary preferences, allergens, NCD status, and optional location data.

For professionals using Lishe Manager, we may collect name, credentials, contact details, and verification documentation.

Automatically collected data may include browser details, device information, session information, and usage logs.

3. Health Data

Lishe processes limited health-related data such as self-declared conditions and nutrition-related inputs provided by users or professionals acting on behalf of users.

We do not store hospital records, integrate with national health databases, or share medical data without consent unless required by law.

4. How We Use Data

We use data to:

  • Generate meal plans and nutrition guidance.
  • Improve algorithms and platform quality.
  • Send reminders, alerts, and operational messages.
  • Support professionals and respond to platform requests.
  • Improve performance, reliability, and service delivery.

We do not sell user data.

5. Data Sharing

We may share data with certified professionals if a user is routed through Lishe Manager, with service providers supporting hosting or messaging infrastructure, or when disclosure is required by law.

6. Data Storage and Security

We implement secure hosting, encrypted HTTPS connections, and access controls for professional dashboards. However, no system is 100 percent secure, and users should also protect their own devices and credentials.

7. Data Retention

We retain data while an account is active, for as long as needed to deliver the service, and as required by law or legitimate operational obligations. Users may request deletion by contacting us.

8. User Rights

You may request correction of inaccurate information, request deletion of your data where applicable, and withdraw consent for marketing communication. Contact info@plateai.africa for privacy requests.

9. International Users

Lishe is currently focused on Tanzania. Data may be processed in secure cloud environments outside Tanzania where service infrastructure or support providers operate.
