About PlateAI

We are committed to creating a healthy eating ecosystem - empowering Tanzanians to eat healthily through simplified personalized meal plans and easily accessible, affordable planned meals. Leveraging a network of food vendors (trained and equipped in Dar es Salaam), we are committed to expanding our impact to the entire country.

At PlateAI, we understand the challenges because we have experienced at some point our relatives and close people facing diet-related issues. This personal connection drives our mission to make healthy eating accessible to everyone.
